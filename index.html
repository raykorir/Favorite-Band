About Electric Groove

Electric Groove is an electrifying band that combines elements of funk, jazz, and electronic music to create a unique and energetic sound. Formed in 2005, they have released several albums and performed at major music festivals around the world.

Albums

Funky Beats

Funky Beats is Electric Groove's debut album, featuring a blend of funky basslines, groovy rhythms, and catchy melodies. It includes hit tracks like "Electric Funk" and "Groove Machine".

Electro Jazz

Electro Jazz takes Electric Groove's sound to new heights with its fusion of electronic beats and jazz improvisation. The album showcases their versatility and experimentation.

Groove Fusion

Groove Fusion is Electric Groove's latest release, featuring a dynamic mix of funk, jazz, and world music influences. It's their most ambitious and diverse album yet.
